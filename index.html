About Me

About Myself

Tanakit Jansom was born in Phichit, Thailand, in 2004. Currently, he is a Ph.D. student at the Bio-Inspired Robotics and Neural Engineering (BRAIN) Lab, School of Information Science and Technology, Vidyasirimedhi Institute of Science and Technology (VISTEC), Thailand. He received his B.Eng. degree in robotics and automation engineering from the King Mongkut's University of Technology North Bangkok, Thailand, in 2026. His research centers on medical robotics, specifically exoskeleton systems for rehabilitation and human movement assistance, combining mechanical design, control systems, and machine learning into a working system.
